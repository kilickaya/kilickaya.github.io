Hello world, I'm Mert Kilickaya!

I'm a first year PhD. student in QUvA labs, The University of Amsterdam. My research interest lies at the intersection of vision and language. Specifically, I am interested in developing visual representations that can be utilized for vision and language tasks, such as image captioning, visual question answering and visual dialogue systems.
