Mert Kilickaya

Hello! I am an AI researcher specializing in deep learning for self-supervised visual representations. My work focuses on developing autonomous systems that continuously improve by extracting supervision from dynamic data.

I have contributed to self-supervised continual learning at the Learning to Learn Lab and earned my PhD in deep vision at the QUvA Lab under Arnold Smeulders.
